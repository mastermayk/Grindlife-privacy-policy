Introduction

Welcome to Grind Life RPG ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using Grind Life RPG, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use our App, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, avatar selection, focus area preferences
  • User-Generated Content: Quest titles, descriptions, notes, guild messages, chat messages
  • Progress Data: XP earned, level progress, achievements unlocked, quest completion history
  • Social Data: Guild memberships, friend connections, challenge participation

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our App:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, screens viewed, interactions
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, app performance metrics
  • Location Data: We do NOT collect precise location data. Only general region for service optimization.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Supabase (Backend Provider): Authentication and database services
  • RevenueCat (Subscription Management): Purchase history, subscription status
  • Google AdMob (Advertising): Ad interaction data (free tier users only)
  • App Stores: Purchase verification, app installation data

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Provide and maintain the App services
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Enable quest creation, tracking, and completion
  • Facilitate guild creation and social features
  • Track progress, XP, levels, and achievements
  • Synchronize data across devices

2.2 Communication

  • Send important account notifications
  • Notify you of guild activities and challenges
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send service announcements and updates

2.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • Conduct research and development
  • Understand user preferences and behavior

2.4 Subscription and Payments

  • Process premium subscription purchases
  • Manage subscription renewals and cancellations
  • Provide access to premium features
  • Handle refund requests

2.5 Advertising (Free Tier Only)

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad effectiveness
  • Provide rewarded ad experiences

3. How We Share Your Information

Important: We do NOT sell your personal information to anyone.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who assist us:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment processing
  • Google AdMob: Ad serving (free tier users only)
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Data storage and hosting

3.2 Within the App

Certain information is visible to other users:

  • Public Profile: Username, avatar, level (visible to friends and guild members)
  • Guild Information: Guild membership, contribution stats, messages in guild chat
  • Leaderboards: Username, level, XP (if you participate)
  • Achievements: Unlocked achievements may be visible to friends

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Password Protection: Passwords hashed and salted
  • Access Controls: Strict limits on data access
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security monitoring
  • Secure Infrastructure: Data stored on secure Supabase servers
Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Obligations: Some data retained longer for compliance
  • Anonymized Data: May retain anonymized data indefinitely

Deleting Your Data

You can delete your account at any time:

  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Select "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm deletion
  4. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

Grind Life RPG is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

If you are under 13, please do not use this App. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.
